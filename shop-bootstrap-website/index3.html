About

The zero waste shop is a UK based shop that is dedicated to reducing the amount of disposable products people use by offering an inexpensive, fashionable and reusable alternative.

Started in 2018 our shop aims to educate people about their daily consumer choices as well as offering them a one-stop-shop for all the products they need to reduce their own waste.

We know that reuseable products can have relatively big upfront costs but save you money in the long run. This is why at the zero waste shop we now have the options to split payments over a few months. When buying the products please click the ‘direct debit’ option at the checkout.
